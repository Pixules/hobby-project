Some Places to check out

No Face Tattoo

Established in November 2018, No Face Tattoo studio is a private Liverpool tattoo studio in Liverpool city centre on London Road, close to Lime Street train station. A spacious and friendly studio capable of taking on a variety of different styles of tattoo to a high standard.

Address

Top Floor of The Tapestry 68-74 Kempston St, Liverpool, L3 8HL

Details

I first met max who works at No Face back in college! And have planned some guest spots as well as future work with him and the studio. A wonderful accessible friendly space.

Learn More

Black Waltz Tattoo Studio

Private tattoo studio based in central Liverpool with several artists, established in 2019. Known for its anime tattoo artists and having international artists as guests on occassion

Address

2nd Floor, 56 Rodney St, Liverpool L1 9AD

Details

I worked with several of the people at Black Waltz before they set up the studio and have personally been tattooed by Ash too! Definitely the place I would recommend if you are looking for an anime tattoo (if you don't come to me!) with world renowned artist Simon K Bell as a resident.

Learn More

Miss Rie's Tattoo Studio

Established in 2013 Miss Rie's is a friendly studio in South Wales. Rie Specialises mainly in colour work and there are a number of other talented artists who work and guest at the accessible studio

Address

7 Caerleon Rd, Newport NP19 7BU

Details

I worked at Miss Rie's studio for almost 2 years, Rie became a great friend and we enjoyed many cups of tea together as well as learning many things together with tattooing that pushed us both forward in our careers. I will always recommend her and her studio to anyone in the South West!
